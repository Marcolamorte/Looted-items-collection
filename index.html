A Virtual Journey Through History's Stolen Treasures

Embark on a groundbreaking digital experience that sheds light on colonial history. The virtual tour takes you on an immersive journey through an extensive archive of cultural artifacts stolen during periods of colonization.

Why This Matters

Our virtual tour aims to educate, provoke thought, and foster dialogue about the lasting impacts of colonialism on cultural heritage. By digitally reuniting these objects with their stories, we hope to contribute to the ongoing conversation about repatriation and cultural preservation.
