The achaemenid reliefs are stone carvings from the Persian Empire, over 2,500 years ago! They tell stories about ancient kings and warriors. Explorers took them in the 1800s, and today, they’re displayed far away in Europe. Fun fact: Persia was one of the most powerful empires ever, and these carvings show off its greatness!
