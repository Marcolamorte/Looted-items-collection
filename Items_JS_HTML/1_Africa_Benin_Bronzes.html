dc:description The "Benin Bronzes" are a group of over a thousand metal plaques and sculptures created by the Edo people of the Kingdom of Benin. These artworks were used to decorate the royal palace in Benin City and often depicted historical figures, noblemen, warriors, and important events from the kingdom's history.
