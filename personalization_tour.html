Tour Personalization

The Looted Items Collection virtual tour is designed to provide a personalized exploration experience tailored to your interests and objectives. Whether you're curious about the historical timeline, the social and political impacts of colonialism, or the geographical journey of these artifacts, this is the start of your personalized journey.

Select one of the tours below to begin your exploration. Each option provides a unique perspective on the artifacts and the stories behind them, allowing you to shape your journey through looted history. For every tour you can choose the lenght and the complexity of the texts representing our collection objects. All the texts have three level of lenght ("short", "medium","long") and complexity ("fun", "basic", "expert"), you can combine all these features according to your preferences. Enjoy!
